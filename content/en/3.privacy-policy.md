# Worket Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 19 June 2026**

At Worket we take your privacy seriously. This Policy explains what personal data we process, for what purposes, on what legal basis, with whom we share it, and what rights you have. It applies to your use of the Worket app.

## 1. Data Controller

The controller of your data is Paul Marquez (a private individual), the owner of Worket. For privacy matters you can contact us at [contact@worketapp.com](mailto:contact@worketapp.com).

## 2. What Data We Process

- **Account data**: your email address and authentication data. If you sign in with Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive the basic data those providers share with us to create your account.
- **Profile data**: first and last name, phone number, location, and, optionally, date of birth, bio, photo, and tags.
- **Location**: an approximate location used to show you nearby offers and to place yours. Other users are never shown your exact position, only an approximate area.
- **Content and activity**: the offers you post or apply to, your chat messages, your ratings, saved items, and applications.
- **Usage data (analytics)**: how you interact with the app (see section 4).
- **Technical and notification data**: app version, language, and the device identifiers needed to send you push notifications if you enable them.

We do not process payment for the work: Worket is not involved in it. Paid subscriptions are handled through Apple, which acts as the controller of that payment data.

## 3. Purposes and Legal Basis

- **Providing the service** (creating your account and profile, posting and applying for offers, showing nearby offers, enabling chat and ratings): legal basis, performance of the contract (the Terms you accept when using the app).
- **Improving the app** through usage analytics: legal basis, your consent / our legitimate interest, which you can withdraw by disabling it at any time (section 4).
- **Sending you notifications**: legal basis, your consent when you enable them on your device.
- **Ensuring security** and preventing fraud or abuse: legal basis, our legitimate interest.
- **Complying with legal obligations** and responding to authorities' requests: legal basis, compliance with a legal obligation.

## 4. Usage Analytics

We use PostHog, a product-analytics provider, to understand how the app is used and to improve it. This analytics collects usage and interaction data (for example, screens viewed, actions taken, offers posted or applied to, searches, and subscription events such as the selected plan and its price), the app version and language, an approximate location derived from your IP address (city or region, never your precise location), and an internal user identifier that links this data to your account once you sign in.

This analytics does not collect sensitive or contact personal data (it does not include your name, email, or phone), nor the content of your messages (we only record that a message was sent and its approximate length, never the text), nor your search terms (only their length). We do not use this data for advertising, do not share it with third parties for advertising, and do not use the advertising identifier (IDFA).

This data is processed on our behalf on servers located in the European Union. You can turn analytics off at any time from Settings → Privacy within the app. If you turn it off, we stop collecting this usage data from your device.

## 5. Who We Share Your Data With

- **With other users**: your public profile (name, photo, bio, tags, and ratings) is visible to other users in the normal operation of the app. Anyone in a chat with you sees the messages in that conversation.
- **With providers that process data on our behalf** (processors): the infrastructure and storage provider that hosts the app and your data, the analytics provider (PostHog), and Apple for notifications and subscriptions.

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

## 6. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we erase or anonymize your data as described in section 8. We may retain certain information for as long as necessary to comply with legal obligations or to handle potential claims.

## 7. Your Rights

You can exercise at any time your rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability, as well as withdraw your consent. You can exercise many of them directly from the app's settings (editing your profile, disabling analytics, deleting your account). For any other request, write to us at [contact@worketapp.com](mailto:contact@worketapp.com).

If you believe we have not handled your rights properly, you can lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency ([www.aepd.es](https://www.aepd.es)) or your local data protection authority.

## 8. Account Deletion

From the menu, under "Cancel / delete account", you can delete your account permanently. When you do, we remove your profile, your photos, your active offers and drafts with their chats, your applications, saved items, notifications, and subscription.

So as not to break other people's conversations, the text of your messages in chats of already-completed offers is kept, but you appear as "Deleted user": your identity is anonymized. This is an irreversible action and, once deleted, you will not be able to sign in again with those credentials.

## 9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is completely foolproof, but we work to reduce risks and protect your information.

## 10. Minors

Worket is intended for people aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly process data of minors below that age.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect legal or service changes. We will inform you of material changes by reasonable means, and the "Last updated" date will indicate the version in force.

## 12. Contact

For any question about this Policy or your data, write to us at [contact@worketapp.com](mailto:contact@worketapp.com).
